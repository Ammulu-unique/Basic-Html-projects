About codehelp

CodeHelp is an online coding education platform founded by Love Babbar that focuses mainly on teaching Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA), Web Development, and interview preparation for students and freshers; it offers structured courses, recorded lectures, practice problems, projects, mock tests, and doubt-support, making it popular among Indian engineering students aiming for placements, especially beginners who want guided learning from basics to job-ready skills, though success still depends on regular practice and self-effort rather than just watching the course.
